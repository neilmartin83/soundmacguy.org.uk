At last week's [London Apple Admins](http://www.londonappleadmins.org.uk/) meet-up, I spoke about how to get started with the [Adobe User Sync Tool](https://github.com/adobe-apiplatform/user-sync.py) (UST). Thanks again to [Steve](https://blog.quirke.org/) and [ThoughtWorks](https://www.thoughtworks.com) for hosting us and [dataJAR](https://datajar.co.uk/) for the delicious food and drink!

In my environment, the UST is the core behind automating the process of getting our 30,000-ish directory users up and running with the identities they need to use in the world of Adobe Creative Cloud. With the advent of Shared Device Licensing, this becomes even more important.
